Ultimatum Game

Introduction

Welcome to the ultimatum game! You are going to play several rounds via a computer network with another partner. At the end of the entire game session, you will be ranked against others based on your overall performance.

There are four sets of games in total, a sum of money ($10/$100/$1,000/$10,000) will be divided between the two players. In each game set, there will be 10 rounds of games.

If the responder ACCEPTS the proposal, the money will be split as proposed.
If the responder REJECTS the proposal, NO ONE will receive any money.
Please only key in integer values.
